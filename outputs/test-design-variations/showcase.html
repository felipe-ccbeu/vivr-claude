6 Estilos de Design

DARK BOLD ⭐

Dark Bold

Palette: Vivr Gradient + #0d0d0d OLED Black

Typography: 36px/900 headline, 14px/500 body

Vibe: Energetic, premium, high-conversion

  • Default para todas as campanhas
  • Gradiente Vivr de 6 cores
  • Multi-layer glow effects
  • Proven performance baseline
MINIMAL CLEAN

Minimal Clean

Palette: #ffffff White + #26c6da Cyan

Typography: 32px/800 headline, 13px/400 body

Vibe: Clarity, minimalist, design-focused

  • Zero gradients, zero glows
  • Thin borders only
  • Maximum whitespace
  • Para públicos design-conscious
EDITORIAL

Editorial

Palette: #f5f3f0 Warm Off-White + Magenta

Typography: 34px/700 Serif, 14px/400 Serif

Vibe: Sophisticated, authoritative, magazine

  • Serif typography (Georgia)
  • Warm off-white background
  • 3px magenta top border
  • Thought leadership vibes
FUTURISTIC

Futuristic

Palette: #0a0e27 Navy + Neon Cyan/Magenta

Typography: 38px/900 UPPERCASE + glow

Vibe: Innovation, tech-forward, high-energy

  • Neon cyan (#00d9ff) + magenta (#a000ff)
  • Dual-layer glow effects
  • Deep navy background
  • Para early adopters & developers
HIGH CONTRAST ♿

High Contrast

Palette: #ffffff White + #000000 Black + Yellow

Typography: 40px/900 headline, 15px/600 body

Vibe: Accessible, readable, bold geometry

  • WCAG AAA compliant (21:1)
  • Golden yellow accent (#ffcc00)
  • Bold geometric borders
  • Maximum readability for all
DARK & WHITE

Dark & White

Palette: #ffffff White + #000000 Black

Typography: 35px/700 headline, 14px/400 body

Vibe: Timeless, elegant, premium

  • Pure monochromatic design
  • Thin 1px borders
  • Geometric dividers
  • Understated luxury

💡 Por Que Isso Importa

Cada design é otimizado para públicos diferentes e objetivos diferentes. Dark Bold é o default (proven conversion), mas Minimal Clean pode performar melhor com designers, Editorial com executivos, Futuristic com tech users, High Contrast com accessibility focus, e Dark White com premium positioning.

Benefício: Uma única copy reutilizável em 6 variações visuais diferentes = A/B testing mais rápido, públicos mais específicos, melhor conversão.
